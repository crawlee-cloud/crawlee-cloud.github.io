# Self-Hosted Scraping Infrastructure vs Bright Data

Bright Data offers bundled proxies and managed scraping. **Crawlee Cloud** provides the orchestration layer you control — with any proxy provider.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Bright Data | Crawlee Cloud |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Proxies** | Bundled (lock-in) | Any provider |
| **Platform** | Managed | Self-Hosted |
| **Target** | Enterprise | Developers |
| **Flexibility** | Their stack | Your stack |

## Why Choose Crawlee Cloud?

- **Bring your own proxies** — Use any provider, no lock-in
- **Self-hosted** — Control your infrastructure
- **Developer-focused** — Code-first approach
- **Open source** — MIT licensed
